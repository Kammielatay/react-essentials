Chadwick Aaron Boseman

Date of Birth: November 29th, 1976

Date of Death: August 28th, 2020

~Chadwick was born in Anderson, South Carolina. He studied directing at Howard University.

~During this time at Howard, he was a student of Phylicia Rashad, who came together with Denzel Washington to fundraise money so that Boseman and a few other students could attend the Oxford Mid-Summer Program of the British American Drama Academy in London.

~It wasn't until 2008 that he moved to LA to pursue his career in acting.

~His breakthrough performance was playing famous baseball player Jackie Robinsdon in 42.

~He continued to play historical roles, such as: James Brown in Get On Up and Thurgood Marshall in Marshall.

~Black Panther gave Boseman international fame from 2016 to 2019

~It came as a surprise to many when news came out regarding his death because he kept his diagnoses with stage IV colon cancer a secret while playing iconic roles.
